Portfolio

CELEBRITY STYLING

Celebrity styling involves curating fashionable looks and outfits for public figures, ensuring they make a statement on red carpets, events, or photo shoots, while aligning with the client's personal style and the occasion's requirements. The goal is to enhance the celebrity's image and create memorable fashion moments.

FASHION EDITORIAL

Editorial styling involves crafting fashion-forward looks and outfits for magazine editorials or fashion spreads, capturing the latest trends and artistic concepts while showcasing creative vision and attention to detail, aiming to create visually striking imagery that resonates with readers and inspires fashion enthusiasts.

THEMATIC STYLING

Thematic styling focuses on creating cohesive outfits and visuals around specific themes or concepts, incorporating elements such as color schemes, patterns, and accessories to convey a particular mood or story, ensuring every aspect of the styling contributes to the overall theme’s impact.
